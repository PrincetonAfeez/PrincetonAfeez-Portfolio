The Technical Chapter

Small apps, serious documentation.

After 35 years operating restaurants, I'm now studying Python and system architecture. I build small applications, most of them command-line tools, some of them web applications, to learn the disciplines that go into shipping software.

Every project ships with five documents: an Architecture Decision Record, a Technical Design Document, an Interface Design Specification, a Runbook, and a Lessons Learned retrospective.

The discipline is the point. The applications themselves are small. The catalog of decisions, designs, interfaces, runbooks, and reflections is the artifact.
